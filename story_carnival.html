Project Description

I developed MotionFlix alongside two other classmates for a final project in Spring 2024. The assignment was to design and implement a website based on a dataset of the top 1000 movies on IMDb. My group and I began by deciding on a general concept: movie education. We wanted to develop a service where users could test their current movie knowledge as well as expand that knowledge by browsing for new movies to watch. Once we had decided on this theme, we completed usability testing to garner what features would be most important to include in a movie education website. We interviewed stakeholders, and we used that information throughout the development process.
