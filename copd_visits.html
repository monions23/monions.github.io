Analyzing COPD-Related ER Visits

Project Description

  • Completed for a lab assignment in GEOG:2500: Foundations of GIS
  • Data regarding Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease (COPD) exported from the Iowa Department of Public Health
    • As a csv
  • Spatial county data taken from the Iowa Department of Transportation
  • Non-spatial data and spatial data joined to form one cohesive dataset for ArcGIS
  • Final map designed in ArcGIS

What I Learned

  • How to obtain and analyze publicly available datasets
  • How to effectively join two datasets together using a common attribute
