About Me

My name is Meredith Onions, and I am a junior studying informatics - which can be defined as computer science from more of a usability standpoint - at the University of Iowa. I love to learn, and I am very passionate about academics. I am particularly intrigued by the intersection between art and technology; I absolutely love to code and think through logical problems, and I also greatly enjoy visual design.

My goal is to have a career that involves innovating and solving problems in the world of technology. I am proficient in many programming languages such as Java and Python. As a part of my major, I have learned a lot about programming and HCI, and as a computer science minor, I have taken coursework such as Discrete Structures, Data Structures, and Algorithms. I developed this website myself to present a little more about me and my work.
