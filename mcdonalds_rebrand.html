McDonald's Rebrand

Project Description

  • Final project for DSGN:2500: Graphic Design I in Fall 2024
  • Redesign of McDonald's logo, business card (front and back), and menu
  • Software used: Adobe Illustrator
  • Theme: travel and transportation
    • Arrow in logo - inspired by arrows on highway road signs
    • Yellow dashed border around text in menu - inspired by lines on road
  • Aimed for simplicity and a cartoonish, youthful look
    • For appeal to children (a key consumer demographic)
    • Motif of red circles - connection to Ronald McDonald's clown nose
  • Kept overall color scheme of red and yellow
    • For more recognizable and familiar rebrand
