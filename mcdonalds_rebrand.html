McDonald's Rebrand

Project Description

  • Final project in DSGN:2500 in Fall 2024
  • Software used: Adobe Illustrator
  • I reflected upon memories of my family going to McDonald’s
    • Realized that we often eat there when we are traveling
  • Theme: travel and transportation
  • Logo
    • Kept the letter M since it is so synonymous with McDonald’s
    • However - instead of the golden arches, my version of the logo is derived from an arrow
      • Inspired by the arrows that one sees on highway road signs
    • Because of its simplicity, I used it for the front of the rebranded business card as well
  • Motif of red circles
    • Reminded me of Ronald McDonald’s clown nose
    • I felt that it gave my rebrand a more youthful look
  • More cartoonish, youthful looking font for business card and menu
    • I felt that it appealed to children (a key demographic for McDonald’s)
  • Yellow dashed border around the text in the menu
    • Meant to represent the dashed lines on a road
  • Kept overall color scheme of red and yellow
      • I felt it would make my rebrand more recognizable and familiar
