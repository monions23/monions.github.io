Highly Detailed Location Photo

Project Description

This piece was done as part of a larger project for Graphic Design I in Fall 2024. We were assigned to recreate a highly detailed location photo in Adobe Illustrator. For my reference image, I chose a picture I took of the bay windows by the train station at Main Street, U.S.A in Disney World. I used the pen tool for all of the line work, and then I used live paint and the recoloring tool to create my color scheme.

I chose teal as the base for my color scheme because it is one of my favorite colors and I felt that it had a calming effect. The two renditions of the piece represent good tint and bad tint; one has higher contrast between the different shades of teal, while the other has lower contrast. I felt that this was a really good exercise in thinking about tints and shades; though I only used teal for this piece, my process taught me a lot about color as a whole.
